Bing Concert Hall

The Bing Concert Hall atStanford will play a vital roleon campus and in the localcommunity. The hall'sexceptional acoustics andstate-of-the -art technical capabilities will showcase theworld's finest performers.The hall is scheduled to open early in 2013. The concert hall will have 844 seats in an intimate vineyard-style configuration, with terraced sections wrapping all the wayaround the stage.
